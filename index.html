What is Trézor Bridge®?

Trézor Bridge® is an essential software utility that acts as a communication bridge between your Trezor™ hardware wallet and desktop browser. It ensures a seamless and secure interaction, enabling users to manage their cryptocurrencies directly from the browser interface without compromising on privacy or performance.

Designed with security at its core, Trézor Bridge® is lightweight, regularly updated, and supports major operating systems. Whether you're sending or receiving crypto assets, checking balances, or managing accounts, Trézor Bridge® guarantees a frictionless experience. With end-to-end encryption and zero telemetry, it's trusted by thousands of users worldwide.

Download Trézor Bridge® today and experience the safest way to connect your Trezor™ device to the digital world.
